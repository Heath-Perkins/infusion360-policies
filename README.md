  <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
  <div class="meta"><strong>Effective date:</strong> May 8, 2026 &nbsp;·&nbsp; <strong>Last updated:</strong> May 8, 2026</div>

  <p>Infusion 360 ("the app", "we", "us") is an educational IV infusion reference and dose calculator for licensed healthcare clinicians. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect, and how the limited information that is processed is handled.</p>

  <h2>Summary</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>No account.</strong> Infusion 360 does not require sign-up or login.</li>
    <li><strong>No personal data collection.</strong> We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, location, photos, health data, or device identifiers for tracking.</li>
    <li><strong>No advertising.</strong> We do not show ads and do not use advertising SDKs.</li>
    <li><strong>No third-party analytics.</strong> We do not use third-party analytics, attribution, or tracking SDKs.</li>
    <li><strong>No tracking across apps or websites.</strong> We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.</li>
    <li><strong>One-time purchase.</strong> Payment is processed by Apple. We never see your payment information.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Information processed by the app</h2>

  <h3>1. Information stored only on your device</h3>
  <p>The following are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage and never leave your device:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Your Favorites (drugs you star for quick access)</li>
    <li>Calculator inputs and results during a session</li>
    <li>App preferences and UI state</li>
  </ul>
  <p>You can remove all of this at any time by deleting the app.</p>

  <h3>2. AI Compatibility Checker (network feature)</h3>
  <p>When — and only when — you use the AI Compatibility Checker, the app sends the following to our backend proxy in order to generate a response:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>The drug names, diluents, and line-type options you select or type</li>
    <li>A request identifier used to return the response</li>
  </ul>
  <p>We do <strong>not</strong> send your name, email, Apple ID, device identifier, IP-derived location, contacts, photos, or any health information about a patient. Do not enter patient-identifying information into the app; the app does not need it and is not designed to receive it.</p>
  <p>The query is processed by our backend, which forwards it to an AI model provider over an encrypted connection. Responses are returned to your device. Queries are not associated with a user account (because there is no account) and are not used to build a profile about you.</p>

  <h3>3. Purchases</h3>
  <p>Infusion 360 is a one-time purchase made through the Apple App Store. Apple processes the transaction and shares only the information needed to verify your purchase entitlement (e.g. an anonymous receipt). We do not receive your name, email address, or payment details from Apple.</p>

  <h2>Information we do NOT collect</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>We do not collect or store personal information.</li>
    <li>We do not collect health information about you or any patient.</li>
    <li>We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, calendar, reminders, or location.</li>
    <li>We do not use cookies, advertising identifiers, or cross-app tracking.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Children</h2>
  <p>Infusion 360 is intended for use by licensed healthcare professionals. It is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.</p>

  <h2>Medical disclaimer</h2>
  <p>Infusion 360 is an educational and reference tool. It is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or monitor patients, and does not connect to infusion pumps or hospital systems. All content is provided to support — not replace — clinical judgment, institutional protocols, current product labeling, and pharmacy consultation. Always verify dosing and compatibility information before clinical use.</p>

  <h2>Security</h2>
  <p>All network requests use HTTPS/TLS. Because the app does not collect accounts or personal data, there is no user profile that could be exposed in the event of a breach of our systems.</p>

  <h2>Data retention</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>On-device data (favorites, preferences) is retained until you delete it or delete the app.</li>
    <li>AI Compatibility queries may be retained by our backend in aggregated, non-identifying logs solely to monitor service health and prevent abuse, and are not associated with any user identity.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Your choices</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>To remove all local data, delete the app from your device.</li>
    <li>To stop sending any data to our backend, simply do not use the AI Compatibility Checker. The rest of the app works fully offline.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Changes to this policy</h2>
  <p>If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, surface the change inside the app or on the App Store listing.</p>

  <h2>Contact</h2>
  <p>Questions about this policy or about Infusion 360 privacy practices?</p>
  <p>Email: <a href="mailto:admin@serenityglobalsolutions.com">admin@serenityglobalsolutions.com</a></p>
